# **Welcome to the Official Dragon Ball Rage Wiki!**

Welcome to the official encyclopedia dedicated to the **Roblox experience** known as **Dragon Ball Rage!** Developed by **iDracius** and released on May 23, 2015, the game has accumulated nearly one billion visits and over two million favorites, while continuing to receive updates for over <span id="game-years" style="font-weight: bold;">0</span> years!

Before you lies an **adventurous epic of your own creation.** Starting from **humble beginnings,** you’ll be given the opportunity to **train your way up** from a **nameless fighter** all the way to a **divine being,** feared and respected across the **entire universe.** Fight your enemies, friends, rivals, unlock epic skills, incredible modes & transformations! Will you become the strongest in this action-packed anime RPG?

Any and all **information** you might need on your way towards meeting this challenge **can be found here,** including a [**link**](https://www.roblox.com/games/71315343/WINTER-Dragon-Ball-Rage) to the game for those just beginning their adventure, consider visiting the [**How To Play**](https://dragonballrage.wiki/how-to-play) page for guidance on getting started.
